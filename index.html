What Is Fixed Piece?

Fixed Piece is a Fan Project of cutting down all unwanted filler from the One Piece episodes. We come from massive inspiration of the other fan project One Pace. However, we make sure that filler is cut out unless it is filler that the fanbase actually wants included. We try to follow the exact order of the panels in the Manga but make sure the viewer experience is still perfect.
